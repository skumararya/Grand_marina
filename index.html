The qualities of
Branded Residences
A unique global asset class
A universal emblem of status, the branded residence traces its history back to New York in the 1920s when the Sherry-Netherland Hotel opened in Manhattan, offering luxury homes to the city's upper class. From gradual growth in the following decades the asset class exploded in the 1980s and today has led to 'starchitects', and fashion, automotive and hospitality brands lending their unique DNA to signature residential projects that continue to captivate the global elite.
